Top 10 INVINCIBLE characters

Welcome to my ranking of the strongest characters in the Invincible universe!

I've ranked these characters based purely on their raw power levels and combat

abilities - not their influence on the story or how much I like them.

Let's dive into who would actually win in a fight!

Top 10

POWERPLEX

POWERS: Powerplex possesses multiple superhuman abilities including enhanced strength, speed, durability, and the ability to generate powerful electrical energy blasts. His powers appear to be augmented by advanced technology integrated into his suit, making him a formidable combatant.
WHY 10: Powerplex ranks at #10 because while his abilities make him a genuine threat, skilled heroes can eventually uncover the true source of his strength â€” the energy absorption from his suit. Once they realize that physical attacks only fuel him, his advantage can be neutralized through strategy and restraint rather than brute force. However, Powerplex has proven especially dangerous against heroes who rely on rage, arrogance, or overwhelming force, as their recklessness only feeds his power and plays directly into his hands.
STORY: Powerplex's origin stems from tragedy - he's driven by a personal vendetta against Invincible after believing the hero was responsible for his family's death during a superhero battle. This grief and rage fuel his relentless pursuit of revenge against the young hero.

Top 9

MONSTER GIRL

POWERS: Monster Girl possesses the unique ability to transform into a massive, powerful monster form with strength rivaling that of Viltrumites. Her monster form grants her incredible durability, enhanced speed, and devastating physical attacks. However, each transformation causes her to age backwards in her human form.
WHY 9: Monster Girl ranks at #9 because while her monster form possesses Viltrumite-level strength, she has significant limitations. She cannot fly like true Viltrumites, is extremely vulnerable in her human form, and her reverse aging creates long-term consequences. Her powers can be countered by targeting her human form or exploiting her lack of flight.
STORY: Amanda, known as Monster Girl, gained her powers through genetic experimentation that cursed her with reverse aging. Despite appearing as a young woman, she's actually much older mentally. She joined the Guardians of the Globe where her monster form proved invaluable in combat, though the tragic cost of growing younger weighs heavily on her.

Top 8

ATOM EVE

POWERS: Atom Eve possesses incredible matter manipulation abilities, allowing her to rearrange matter at the molecular level. She can create energy barriers, reconstruct objects, transmute materials, and even heal injuries by reorganizing damaged tissue. Her powers are theoretically limitless but are restricted by mental blocks for her own safety.
WHY 8: Atom Eve ranks at #8 because while her matter manipulation is potentially reality-altering, she has significant limitations. Enemies can kill her before she accesses her unlimited power, she's constrained by human reaction time, and requires hand gestures to use her abilities unless in her ultimate form. This makes her vulnerable to restraint attacks.
STORY: Samantha Eve Wilkins gained her powers through government experiments before her birth, making her a living weapon. She escaped her intended purpose and became a hero, joining the Teen Team and later developing a close relationship with Mark Grayson. Her journey involves learning to overcome her mental limitations and embrace her true potential.

Top 7

THE IMMORTAL

POWERS: The Immortal possesses true immortality, having lived for thousands of years with superhuman strength, speed, durability, and flight. He can resurrect from death and regenerate, but needs approximately 70% of his body mass to regenerate independently. With less mass, he requires help to reattach body parts, though he can regenerate his head under the right conditions.
WHY 7: The Immortal ranks at #7 because while his immortality makes him extremely difficult to kill permanently, he has significant limitations. He struggles with anger issues and isn't as wise as expected since he barely remembers his past lives, making him less skilled in combat strategy. He can be temporarily defeated through overwhelming force.
STORY: The Immortal has lived for millennia, witnessing the rise and fall of civilizations. He served as Abraham Lincoln and has been a protector of Earth for centuries. As leader of the Guardians of the Globe, his vast experience and unwavering dedication make him a formidable hero, though his trust was shattered by Omni-Man's betrayal.

Top 6

ALLEN THE ALIEN

POWERS: Allen the Alien possesses incredible superhuman strength, speed, durability, flight capabilities, and telepathic abilities. His most unique ability is adaptive evolution - each time he's injured or defeated, his body adapts and becomes stronger, developing resistance to whatever harmed him. This makes him progressively more powerful over time.
WHY 6: Allen ranks at #6 because his power level sits just below experienced strong Viltrumites. While his adaptive evolution makes him incredibly dangerous over time, he needs to be injured first to develop his resistances. He can be defeated before his adaptations kick in, and his power growth requires recovery time between battles.
STORY: Allen serves as a Coalition of Planets agent, tasked with evaluating planetary defenses across the galaxy. Initially mistaking Earth for Urath, he becomes an ally to Mark Grayson and Earth's heroes. His dedication to protecting civilizations from Viltrumite expansion makes him a valuable ally in the fight against galactic threats.

Top 5

ROBOT/RUDY

POWERS: Robot possesses genius-level intellect that rivals the greatest minds in the universe. He can transfer his consciousness between multiple robotic bodies, giving him effective immortality and the ability to be in multiple places at once. His advanced technology allows him to create powerful weapons, armor, and strategic battle plans.
WHY 5: Robot ranks at #5 because his intelligence and technological resources make him incredibly dangerous through preparation and strategy. Since he never really has to fight directly and can control his robots remotely, he's always ready to come back with new bodies. However, he can be countered by overwhelming speed attacks or targeting his base.
STORY: Rudolph Connors was a brilliant but dying young man who transferred his consciousness into robotic bodies to survive. As Robot, he became a founding member of the new Guardians of the Globe, using his intellect to coordinate team strategies. He later obtains a human body to get closer to Monster Girl, developing a romantic relationship that drives much of his character development throughout the series.

Top 4

INVINCIBLE

POWERS: Invincible possesses classic Viltrumite abilities including superhuman strength, speed, flight, and near-invulnerability. As a half-Viltrumite, his powers continue to grow stronger with age and experience. He has incredible durability, can survive in space, and possesses enhanced healing. His strategic mind and adaptability make him formidable in combat.
WHY 4: Invincible ranks at #4 because while he has full Viltrumite potential, he's still young and developing his abilities. His power level continues to grow but hasn't reached the peak of experienced Viltrumite warriors. He can be overwhelmed by opponents with more combat experience or those who exploit his emotional attachments and moral constraints.
STORY: Mark Grayson discovered his Viltrumite heritage as a teenager when his powers manifested. Son of the legendary Omni-Man, he initially believed he was protecting Earth alongside his father. After learning the truth about Viltrumite conquest, Mark chose to defend Earth and humanity, becoming a symbol of hope despite overwhelming challenges.

Top 3

CONQUEST

POWERS: Conquest possesses the full range of Viltrumite abilities including immense superhuman strength, speed, flight, and near-invulnerability. As an ancient Viltrumite warrior with centuries of combat experience, his power level far exceeds younger Viltrumites. His battle-hardened physiology and tactical expertise make him a devastating opponent.
WHY 3: Conquest ranks at #3 because he represents peak Viltrumite combat prowess with centuries of warfare experience. However, his prosthetic hand is less powerful than Viltrumite flesh and can be easily destroyed when fighting characters at his power level, creating a significant weakness that can be exploited by equally strong opponents.
STORY: Conquest is an ancient Viltrumite warrior sent to Earth to complete the conquest that Omni-Man failed to finish. With centuries of battle experience across the galaxy, he represents the Viltrumite Empire's relentless expansion. His arrival on Earth brings devastating consequences and tests the limits of Earth's defenders.

Top 2

BATTLE BEAST

POWERS: Battle Beast possesses incredible superhuman strength that rivals or exceeds Viltrumites, along with enhanced speed, durability, and regenerative abilities. His razor-sharp claws can pierce through Viltrumite skin, giving him a unique advantage that even Viltrumites have no natural protection against in combat.
WHY 2: Battle Beast ranks at #2 because his combat prowess, strength, and deadly claws that can cut through Viltrumite flesh put him above almost every other character. However, he cannot fly like Viltrumites, his obsession with worthy opponents can be exploited, and his warrior's code can be used against him strategically in combat situations.
STORY: Battle Beast is a legendary warrior from another planet who lives solely for combat and the thrill of finding worthy opponents. He initially came to Earth seeking a challenge but found most heroes lacking. His respect is earned only through strength and combat skill, making him both a formidable enemy and potential ally.

Top 1

OMNI-MAN

POWERS: Omni-Man possesses the full spectrum of Viltrumite abilities at their peak level, including immense superhuman strength, speed, flight, and near-invulnerability. With over 2,000 years of combat experience and training, his power level represents the absolute pinnacle of what a Viltrumite warrior can achieve through age and experience.
WHY 1: Omni-Man ranks at #1 because he represents peak Viltrumite power with millennia of experience fighting beings at his power level. His raw speed and overwhelming strength are often enough to defeat gimmick-based characters who rely on special abilities rather than pure combat prowess. Few opponents can match his combination of power and experience.
STORY: Nolan Grayson came to Earth as part of the Viltrumite Empire's expansion plan, but spent decades living among humans as a protector. His time on Earth complicated his mission as he developed genuine connections, particularly with his family. The conflict between his Viltrumite duty and his love for Earth creates his most compelling drama.
